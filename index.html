About the Lion Battalion

The Olympic Heights MCJROTC Lion Battalion is dedicated to developing leadership, discipline, and civic responsibility in high school students through military structure and values. Since 2015, our cadets have demonstrated pride, purpose, and professionalism in everything they do.

Our Clubs & Teams

  • Color Guard: Represents the battalion at ceremonies and school events with precision and pride.
  • Marksmanship (Mashmasher): Teaches firearm safety, discipline, and competitive shooting skills.
  • Drill Team: Practices military drill routines and competes with excellence.

Upcoming Events

Stay informed about our special events and activities. Announcements are updated regularly!

  • 🎉 Military Ball: A formal event celebrating our cadets with honor, tradition, and style.
  • 🏃 Field Day: A fun-filled day of games, challenges, and camaraderie for all cadets.
  • 🎖️ Marines Birthday Ceremony: A proud celebration of USMC history and legacy.
  • 📸 TECOM & Competition Days: Watch our cadets compete and grow as a team.
